# User Personas & User Journeys

## 1. The Global Donor (Generous George)
**Profile:**
- Age: 25-45
- Tech-savvy, holds crypto (Solana).
- motivated by transparency and direct impact.
- Frustrated by traditional charity fees and lack of updates.

**Goal:**
Donate quickly to verified causes and see exactly where the money goes.

**User Journey:**
1.  **Discover:** Lands on Homepage via social media link. Sees "Crowdfunding Platform" and "SOL Raised" stats.
2.  **Browse:** Filters campaigns by "Disaster Relief".
3.  **Evaluate:** Clicks a campaign. Checks "Verified" badge and reads the story.
4.  **Action:** Clicks "Donate Now". Connects Phantom Wallet.
5.  **Transaction:** Enters 1 SOL. Approves transaction.
6.  **Confirmation:** Sees success confetti and transaction hash.
7.  **Follow-up:** Checks back later to see progress bar update.

## 2. The Local Changemaker (Community Sita)
**Profile:**
- Age: 20-50
- NGO leader or community activist in Nepal.
- Needs funds for immediate cause (medical, school, rebuilding).
- May have limited tech access but has a smartphone.

**Goal:**
Raise funds globally without complex banking barriers.

**User Journey:**
1.  **Register:** Connects wallet (or creates one via guide).
2.  **Create:** Clicks "Start a Campaign". Fills in details (Title, Description, Goal).
3.  **Verify:** Uploads NGO registration/ID proofs.
4.  **Promote:** Shares campaign link on Facebook/WhatsApp.
5.  **Receive:** Funds arrive directly in wallet as donors contribute.
6.  **Withdraw:** Off-ramps SOL to local currency (via local exchange/p2p).

## 3. The Beneficiary (Direct Recipient)
**Profile:**
- The ultimate receiver of aid (e.g., student needing scholarship, patient).
- Often represented by the Campaign Creator.

**Goal:**
Receive help transparently.

**User Journey (Indirect):**
1.  **Validation:** Provides photos/docs to Campaign Creator.
2.  **Impact:** Receives goods/services purchased with funds.
3.  **Feedback:** Provides success story/photo for Campaign Update.

# Key Metrics (Analytics Plan)
- **Conversion Rate:** % of visitors who connect wallet.
- **Donation Conversion:** % of connected wallets that donate.
- **Average Donation:** Average SOL amount per txn.
- **Retention:** % of donors returning within 30 days.
- **Campaign Success Rate:** % of campaigns reaching 100% goal.
